International Conference on Intelligent Systems for Complex Industrial Processes

Shenyang, Liaoning, China | June 28-29, 2025

Welcome

The 1st International Conference on Intelligent Systems for Complex Industrial Processes will be held on June 28–29, 2025, in the historic city of Shenyang—known as “the birthplace of one imperial lineage and the capital of two dynasties.” by the State Key Laboratory of Comprehensive Automation for Process Industries at Northeastern University to advance the integrated development of automation and intelligence. More than 60 renowned experts and scholars from 17 countries and regions—including the United States, Canada, and the United Kingdom—have been invited (among them over 10 academicians, and more than 30 IEEE Fellows and IFAC Fellows).

Conference Topics

  • Industrial process intelligent sensing and modeling
  • Computational intelligence
  • AI-driven cooperative autonomous control
  • AI-driven industrial process operational optimal decision-making
  • AI-driven abnormal situation monitoring and prediction

    • About Shenyang

    Shenyang is a cultural metropolis that blends history and modernity. It is a national historical and cultural city and the birthplace of Qing Dynasty. After the establishment of the People's Republic of China,Shenyang became an important heavy industry base in China, mainly focused on equipment manufacturing. It has a long history and rich cultural heritage, and is renowned for its magnificent royal architecture,brilliant opera performances, and unique cuisine.
