International Conference on Intelligent Systems for Complex Industrial Processes

Shenyang, Liaoning, China | June 22-23, 2025

Mission

Intelligent systems have become crucial in addressing the complexities of industrial processes, especially in the context of increasing automation and data-driven decision-making. These systems are pivotal for optimizing operations, improving efficiency, and ensuring sustainability across various industries. As industrial systems grow more intricate, the need for advanced intelligent solutions that can adapt to dynamic environments and integrate diverse data sources has never been more urgent.

The objective of this workshop is to offer a collaborative platform for researchers to exchange ideas and showcase the latest advancements in intelligent systems for complex industrial processes. Featuring invited talks from leading experts, the workshop aims to foster knowledge sharing and stimulate discussions on cutting-edge research in this field. We believe that this workshop will serve as an excellent opportunity for both researchers and practitioners to explore innovative solutions, furthering the development of intelligent systems and advancing the state-of-the-art in complex industrial processes.

    • About Shenyang

    Shenyang is a cultural metropolis that blends history and modernity. It is a national historical and cultural city and the birthplace of Qing Dynasty. After the establishment of the People's Republic of China, Shenyang became an important heavy industry base in China, mainly focused on equipment manufacturing. It has a long history and rich cultural heritage, and is renowned for its magnificent royal architecture, brilliant opera performances, and unique cuisine. Whether its delicious food or experiencing historical culture, Shenyang can bring you unforgettable experiences.
